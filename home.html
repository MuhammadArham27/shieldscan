What is ShieldScan

Your personal
URL safety analyzer

ShieldScan is a free, AI-enhanced URL security tool that helps individuals, businesses, and security teams verify whether any web link is safe before clicking. Paste any URL — from emails, messages, social media, or ads — and get an instant threat verdict backed by a 10-point analysis engine.

Threat Intelligence

What we detect

ShieldScan's analysis engine is built to catch the most common and dangerous URL-based attack vectors used against everyday users.

🎣
Phishing
URLs mimicking legitimate services to steal usernames, passwords, and payment data.
🦠
Malware Delivery
Links that silently download malicious software, ransomware, or exploit kits to your device.
🎭
Homograph Attacks
Unicode lookalike characters used to create undetectable spoofed domains (e.g. аpple.com).
✂️
Shortened URLs
URL shorteners that hide the true destination — commonly used to bypass security filters.
⌨️
Typosquatting
Domains with deliberate misspellings of trusted brands to catch mistyped addresses.
🔄
Redirect Chains
Multi-hop redirects through seemingly safe domains to reach malicious final destinations.
🔢
IP-Based URLs
Raw IP addresses used instead of domain names — a classic indicator of no legitimate hosting.
🌐
Risky TLDs
Top-level domains like .xyz, .tk, .ml, and .pw are disproportionately used in scam campaigns.
